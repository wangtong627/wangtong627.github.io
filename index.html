Biography个人简介

About关于我

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Key Laboratory of New Generation Artificial Intelligence Technology and Its Interdisciplinary Applications of Ministry of Education, Southeast University.

I am also a visiting Ph.D. student at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI).

我目前是东南大学教育部新一代人工智能技术及其交叉应用重点实验室的博士研究生。

同时，我也是阿联酋人工智能大学（MBZUAI）的访问博士生。

Research Interests:研究兴趣：

  • Multimodal Models:多模态模型： Vision-Language Models, Agentic AI, LLM Reasoning视觉语言模型，智能体 AI，大语言模型推理
  • Generative AI:生成式 AI： Autoregressive Models, Diffusion Models, Efficient Generative Models, World Models, Video Generation自回归模型，扩散模型，高效生成模型，世界模型，视频生成
  • Medical Image Analysis:医学图像分析： Image Segmentation, Medical Video Analysis, Medical Image Representation Learning图像分割，医学视频分析，医学图像表征学习

I welcome research discussions and collaborations around multimodal models, generative AI, and medical image analysis.
If our interests overlap, feel free to contact me at tong.wang AT mbzuai.ac.ae. 欢迎围绕多模态模型、生成式 AI 和医学图像分析开展交流与合作。
如果我们的研究兴趣契合，欢迎通过 tong.wang AT mbzuai.ac.ae 联系我。
